Ingredients:
Bread
Egg
Salt
Milk

Take the egg, crack it open and just beat it (not the MJ type). Add some milk in the egg
for extra goodness, and while you're at it, add salt, swaadanusar (as per taste).
Now, pre-heat your pan (not the PUBG one). Once heated, dip the bread in the egg-milk-salt mixture
and put it in the pan. Pro Tip: If there are hungry people around, two bread slices can be cooked
to minimize time taken from start to end. After a minute or two, when the bread looks brownish
or golden brown, you have yourself a healthy meal. You could also add pepper, if you feel
the need to. The dish you successfully (or unsuccessfully, I won't judge) made is a good base to
fine-tune to your own taste, by adding various flavours such as tomato (sauce), oregano,
mayonneise and mint chutney (definitely not inspired from Alsa Mall). Bone apple tea or something, idk.
